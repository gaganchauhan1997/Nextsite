About Us

The story of a bus, a metro gate, and a whole lot of street-food love.

Street-food favourites, with a twist.

Food Bus Of India serves India's street-food favourites with a twist — bus-themed dining at Delhi metro stations. Three buses and counting: Lajpat Nagar, Rajinder Nagar and South Extension, with 10,000+ combined Google reviews.
